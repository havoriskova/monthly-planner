About this webpage

The most useful thing for planning - at least for me - is a monthly planner. I used to have pretty and large diaries, but soon during the year I´ve noticed that I hardly open them. I was also lazy to turn the pages to see if I haven´t forget something to do in the previous weeks. Monthly planner helped me a lot with organizing stuff, because it is simply just one page. It feels lighter and more fresh than just enormous amount of pages.

Since my love for monthly planners is eternal and I am learning how to code, I decided to make a monthly planner generator. If you strugle with your design, just look in the gallery for an inspiration!
